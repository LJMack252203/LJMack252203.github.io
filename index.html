chili

  • Time: 1hr 40min
  • Serves: 6 to 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 2 lbs
chopped garlic 2 cloves
tomato sauce 8 ounce
chili powder 2 Tablespoons
ground cumin 1 teaspoon
ground oregano 1 teaspoon
salt 1 teaspoon
cayenne pepper 1/4 teaspoon
masa harina 1/4 cup
kidney beans, drained and rinsed 15 ounce can
pinto beans, drained and rinsed 15 ounce can

Step 1: Place the ground beef in a large pot and throw in the garlic. Cook over medium heat until browned. Drain off the excess fat, and then pour in the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt and cayenne. Stir together well, cover, and then reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. If the mixture becomes overly dry, add 1/2 cup water at a time as needed.

Step 2: After an hour, place the masa harina in a small bowl. Add 1/2 cup water and stir together with a fork. Dump the masa mixture into the chili. Stir together well, and then taste and adjust the seasonings. Add more masa paste and/or water to get the chili to your preferred consistency, or to add more corn flavor. Add the beans and simmer for 10 minutes.

Chicken Alfredo

  • Time: 35 mins
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 tablespoons
bonless chicken breast halves 1 1/4 pounds
salt 1/2 teaspoon
ground black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
prego Roasted Garlic Parmesan Alfredo Sauce 1 jar
fettuccine pasta 1/2 of 1 pound

Step 1: Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle the chicken with the salt and pepper. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until the chicken is cooked through, stirring occasionally. Remove the chicken from the skillet and keep warm. Reduce the heat to medium.

Step 2: Stir the Alfredo sauce in the skillet and heat through. Add the chicken and fettuccine and toss to coat.

Hamburger Soup

  • Time: 35 mins
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 1 lb
onion diced 1 onion
garlic clove 2 cloves
potatoes 2 potatoes
beef broth 3 1/2 cups
diced tomatoes with juice 28 ounces
tomato soup 1 condensed can
Worcestershire sauce 2 teaspoon
Italian seasoning 1 teaspoon
mixed vegetables 3 cups

Step 1: Brown onion, ground beef and garlic until no pink remains. Drain any fat..

Step 2: Add potatoes, broth, tomatoes, tomato soup, Worcestershire sauce, seasoning and bay leaves. Simmer covered 10 minutes.

Step 3: Stir in vegetables. Simmer 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
